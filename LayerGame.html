LAYERING TIPS & TRICKS

Expert advice for mastering the art of layering

01

START THIN, END THICK

Always begin with your thinnest, most fitted layers closest to your body and gradually increase thickness as you move outward. This creates a streamlined silhouette and prevents bunching.

02

PLAY WITH LENGTHS

Vary the lengths of your layers to create visual interest. Try a longer base layer under a shorter mid layer, or a longer coat over a cropped jacket for a contemporary look.

03

MIX TEXTURES

Combine different textures like smooth cotton, soft wool, rugged denim, and technical fabrics to add depth and dimension to your layered outfit.

04

CONSIDER PRACTICALITY

Choose layers that can be easily removed or added as temperatures change. Opt for pieces with zippers, buttons, or other easy-access closures for maximum versatility.
